## Integration Findings (Documentation-Only)

### INT-01: Phase 13 SUMMARY misdeclares separable:true source
- Phase 13 SUMMARY `requires` block claims Phase 12 provides "separable: true flags on 19 verbs"
- Actual: `separable: true` on 19 verbs predates Phase 12 (pre-existing flags)
- Impact: Zero functional. Phase 13 consumed the pre-existing flags correctly.

### INT-02: REQUIREMENTS.md AUDIT-01 verb count
- REQUIREMENTS.md says "All 20 inseparable prefix verbs" — Phase 12 research confirmed 17 is correct
- geben/gehen/gewinnen correctly excluded (ge- is part of stem, not an inseparable prefix)
- Impact: Zero functional. Data is correct at 17.

### INT-03: REQUIREMENTS.md PERF-01/02/03 verb count
- REQUIREMENTS.md says "All 148 German verbs" — Phase 13 correctly targets 144 non-verbphrase entries
- 4 verbphrases are explicitly Out of Scope per REQUIREMENTS.md
- Impact: Zero functional. 144/144 entries satisfied.

### INT-04: CONTEXT.md deviation in noun declension format
- CONTEXT.md specified separate article/noun fields (`{ article: "der", noun: "Hund" }`)
- Implementation uses combined strings (`{ definite: "der Hund", indefinite: "ein Hund" }`)
- PLAN explicitly specifies this format and schema accepts it — intentional override
- Impact: Zero functional. Downstream consumers (Leksihjelp) display combined strings.
